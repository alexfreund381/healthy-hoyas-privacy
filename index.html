Privacy Policy

Healthy Hoyas
Effective Date: August 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and username
  • Age, Weight logs, Sleep Logs, Steps Logs, and Food/Nutrition Logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Healthy Hoyas account
  • To log meals and track nutrition progress
  • To provide core app functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, with encryption in transit and at rest. Only you can access your account information.

4. Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time directly in the Healthy Hoyas app. Deleting your account will permanently remove all associated data, including your email, username, weight logs, and food/nutrition logs, from our servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
